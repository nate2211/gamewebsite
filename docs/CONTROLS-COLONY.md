# Colony and Survival Controls

## Inventory

- Press `E` once to open inventory.
- Press `E` again to close it.
- Use the Colony tab to inspect station status and shared storage.
- `Escape` opens the pause screen.

## Farming

1. Equip a hoe.
2. Right-click grass, dirt, or frozen grass to till it.
3. Equip wheat seeds.
4. Right-click the top of farmland to plant.
5. Wait for four crop stages, then harvest.

## Fishing

1. Equip the fishing rod.
2. Aim at water and right-click.
3. Wait for the bobber to dip.
4. Left-click while the HUD shows `BITE!`.

## Colony

1. Craft a worker box at the crafting table.
2. Put it in the hotbar.
3. Place it on open ground.
4. The assigned colonist appears and starts working.
5. Open Inventory → Colony to inspect progress and storage.
